About Me

After high school, I explored various studies, of which none really grabbed my interest. However, in one of my previous studies, there was a class in which we worked with HTML and CSS. This was hands down the most enjoyable class I took that year. Unfortunately, the rest of the education was focused primarily on topics like marketing, which didn't resonate with me. So, I made the decision to quit.

Since I loved the class that involved creating simple websites, I decided to give web design and development a shot. I enrolled in the course "Web Engineer" at the College of MultiMedia in Amsterdam, which covered both web design principles and building websites with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Quickly, I realized it was the right path for me.

After completing the course, I wanted to delve deeper into the technical aspects of web development. That's when I started a year-long online course on full-stack development.

Alongside pursuing my newfound passion, I began working on small projects on the side. These projects started small, but gradually I took on bigger ones, eventually helping multiple friends and acquaintances set up and build their websites. Currently, I'm collaborating with a couple of friends on developing a mobile app.

I enjoy learning new things and broadening my horizons, not only on the technical aspects of developing, but also in my personal life. Operating in a team is something I particularly enjoy, as there's always something to learn from collaborating with others. I am constantly striving for improvement and always open to feedback.
